<threat_model>
## Trust Boundaries

| Boundary | Description |
|---|---|
| Public material to hand-verified registry | Source pages and transcripts may drift, be misread, or not support a claim. |
| Registry to SQLite | Fixture text is untrusted until provenance, source, and location validation succeeds. |
| SQLite to generated module | Raw text and metadata must not leak into the browser model. |

## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Severity | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| T-02-01 | Tampering | seed-records.json | high | mitigate | Validate each fixture through the shared contract and build into a temporary DB before atomic replacement. |
| T-02-02 | Repudiation | public evidence provenance | high | mitigate | Retain canonical URL, retrieval date, content hash, exact quote, and locator for every retained fact. |
| T-02-03 | Elevation of privilege | reporting source | medium | mitigate | Persist reporting as a separate source kind and prohibit it from becoming primary legal/status truth. |
| T-02-04 | Denial of service | concurrent seed runs | low | mitigate | Fail a second writer with an explicit local lock rather than allow competing SQLite writes. |
</threat_model>
